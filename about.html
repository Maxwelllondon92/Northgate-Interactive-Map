Save/Load Function
Since all notes, color-assignments and other data are stored in your browser, cleaning your cache will erase everything. In the 'How to Use' page is possible to download all data and reload it after a cache wipe.

Why an interactive map?

This interactive website was made as a tool for DM, in order to navigate such a big city map with ease.
Also, it gives more depth to the map itself, adding dedicated section for the lore and details of the city, districts, suburbs and buildings.
Even if built in HTML and working fine if uploaded online, this website was made with the idea to be downloaded and used offline.
